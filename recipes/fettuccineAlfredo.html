Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

Fetticcune pasta tossed with chesse, butter, and cream to make a delicious italian dish

Ingredients

  • 24 ounces of fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 pint heavy cream
  • salt and pepper
  • Garlic salt
  • Romano and Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Add fettuccine to a pot of boiling, lightly salted water and cook for eight to ten minutes
  2. Melt the butter into the cream over low heat
  3. Add salt, pepper, and a dash of garlic salt
  4. Increase to medium heat, stir in 3/4 cup of grated Romano cheese and 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese until melted and sauce has thickened
  5. Add the fettuccine to sauce and toss until coated
